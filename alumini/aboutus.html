Who We Are

The Student-Alumni Relations department fosters a strong connection between current students and alumni. We provide resources and opportunities for students to learn from the experiences of alumni, and for alumni to stay connected with their alma mater.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To connect current students and alumni to create a lifelong network of support and mentorship.

Vision

To empower students to achieve their academic and professional goals through meaningful interactions with alumni.

Our Team

Our dedicated team is passionate about building strong relationships between students and alumni.
